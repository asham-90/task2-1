sinjil-ramallah

Sinjil town is located to the north of Ramallah city, about 21 km away, connecting the north to the south and between the governorates of Ramallah and Nablus. It is located on a high hill with a maximum height above sea level of 860 meters and a minimum height above sea level of 680 meters. Sinjil lands are surrounded by the lands of the villages and towns of: Qaryut, Turmus Ayya, Al-Lubban Ash-Sharqiya, Abwein, Aroura, Jaljalya, and Al-Mazraa Ash-Sharqiya.
